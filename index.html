Move Operations to the Cloud

To keept the company operating during the Covid pandemic under extreme cyber-attacks on their legacy systems. I initiated and executed this project to enable staff to work from home with minimal impact to business operations.

I inherited an ecosystem with antiquated hardware, an in-house data center with over 600 servers in poor health (no one at the company could manage it and business was entirely reliant on a former employee working as a contractor), and no employee could work remotely due to the technical limitations.

While challenging and with plenty of bumps in the road, this project completed in 2 months moving the entire company remote with zero down-time and reduced the server count from 600 to twelve, provisioned via IAC (infrastructure as code) and completely managed in-house.

Modernize Legacy Infrastructure

This project yielded a 280x improvement in deployment and management efficiency by migrating manual processes to IAC (Infrastructure as Code).
